# CoreReceptionAI Overview

## What We Are

AI consulting firm providing workflow automation, AI agents, data analytics, CRM integrations, and SOP automation for small-to-medium businesses.

**We are NOT an AI receptionist service.** We build AI systems for businesses.

## Founded
November 2024

## Current Stage
Pre-revenue, building infrastructure and positioning
