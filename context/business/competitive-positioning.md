# Competitive Positioning

## Market Landscape

### Generic Automation Agencies
- **Who:** Zapier consultants, general VAs
- **Weakness:** Shallow technical depth, point solutions
- **Our Edge:** Deep n8n expertise, integrated systems

### AI Receptionist Services
- **Who:** Smith.ai, Ruby, AI-specific vendors
- **Weakness:** Black box, limited customization, expensive
- **Our Edge:** Custom prompts, full integration, transparency

### DIY Platforms
- **Who:** VAPI direct, Retell direct, n8n themselves
- **Weakness:** Requires technical skill, time investment
- **Our Edge:** Done-for-you implementation, expertise

### Big Consulting Firms
- **Who:** Accenture, Deloitte, etc.
- **Weakness:** Expensive, slow, enterprise-focused
- **Our Edge:** SMB focus, fast, affordable

## Win/Loss Patterns

### We Win When
- Client values customization
- Integration is important
- They want to understand what they're buying
- ROI is clear and measurable
- They've been burned by black-box solutions

### We Lose When
- Client wants cheapest option
- They need enterprise scale
- Decision-maker is non-technical and uncomfortable
- Very short timeline (<2 weeks)
- Budget under $1,000
