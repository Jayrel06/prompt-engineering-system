## Customization Notes

### For Different Project Types

**Workflow Automation:**
- Emphasize time savings
- Include before/after process maps
- ROI = hours saved × hourly rate

**AI Receptionist:**
- Emphasize missed call recovery
- Include call volume estimates
- ROI = missed calls × conversion rate × customer value

**Analytics/Dashboards:**
- Emphasize decision-making improvement
- Include sample visualizations
- ROI = better decisions, hard to quantify

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Client name spelled correctly?
- [ ] Situation accurately reflects discovery call?
- [ ] Scope is clear (included and excluded)?
- [ ] Timeline is realistic?
- [ ] Pricing aligns with discussed budget?
- [ ] ROI calculation is defensible?
- [ ] Next steps are clear?
- [ ] Professional formatting?
